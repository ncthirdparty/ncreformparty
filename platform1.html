Platform Of The Justice For All Party Of North Carolina (In The State)

BASIC HUMAN RIGHTS

  • Codify housing, food, healthcare, and clean water access as basic human rights.
  • Establish net neutrality.

CRIMINAL JUSTICE

  • Prosecute and fine state-based companies that aid and abet in the execution of war crimes..

ECONOMY

  • Increase corporate taxation.
  • Establish the State Infrastructure Bank.
  • Establish a state-wide high-speed rail system and state-owned railroad corporation.
  • Establish a state-owned utilities, including widespread free and fast public internet.
  • Eliminate private utility corporations.
  • Establish a state-owned marijuana distributor following the ABC model with local and small- scale farm ownership.
  • Promote small and worker-owned businesses through tax-relief, rebates, interest-free loans, and public grant money.
  • Reform farm and child labor laws to protect workers.

EDUCATION

  • Establish free public education from preschool through college.
  • Reform the structural issues in public education that disadvantage students on the basis of their race or class.
  • Redefine the mandate of No Child Left Behind to benefit students and improve the quality of their education.

ENVIRONMENT

  • Distribute climate reparations (through the NC Investigatory Commission).
  • Fully transition to renewable resources with an immediate end to oil extraction.
  • Codify rights for non-human entities in collaboration with the Cherokee people.

HEALTH

  • Protect and codify abortion access.
  • Establish social and community intervention agencies for mental health crises.
  • Establish a North Carolina public health insurance option primarily funded by the taxation of healthcare profits.
  • Establish free health care for all.

POLITICS

  • Protect third parties and independent candidates by establishing ranked-choice voting, fusion voting, and public financing of North Carolina elections.
  • End Citizen's United.
  • Create a mandatory, paid holiday for all workers on Election Day.
  • Enfranchise voters through the expansion of polling places, early voting, vote-by-mail, and other related accommodations.
  • Place intentional term limits on career political officials.

SOCIAL JUSTICE

  • Establish the NC Investigatory Commission to distribute reparations to communities harmed by American imperialism. The commission would be made up of people within the communities affected.
  • Distribute reparations to American Descendants of Slavery (through the NC Investigatory Commission).
  • Distribute reparations to indigenous peoples (through the NC Investigatory Commission).
  • Reverse the tides of mass incarceration. Decarcerate, distribute reparations, and expunge petty offenses (through the NC Investigatory Commission on Prison Abolition).
  • Abolish private prisons.
  • Expunge records and release petty marijuana offenders.
  • Demilitarize NC police departments.
  • Reduce bloated police budgets and redirect the funds to create community mental health and intervention services.
  • Establish transformative justice and promote local mediation outside of the judicial system.

WORKERS' RIGHTS

  • Make NC a union state again. Repeal the prohibition of public sector collective bargaining and revoke "right-to-work" laws.
  • Raise the minimum wage to $20 to reflect the cost of living, and continue to adjust it as the cost of living changes.
  • Establish a Workers' Bill of Rights.
  • End taxation on the working class.
