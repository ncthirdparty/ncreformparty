Platform Of The Justice For All Party Of North Carolina (In The Nation)

ECONOMY

  • Establish a National Infrastructure Bank.
  • Nationalize oil and gas corporations.
  • Nationalize railroad transport corporations.
  • Nationalize a high-speed rail system connecting major American metropolises.

ENVIRONMENT

  • Establish the National Climate Reparations Investigatory Commission.
  • Call for an immediate end to oil extraction.

HEALTH

  • Establish universal single payer healthcare.

POLITICS

  • Defund the CIA entirely. Prevent further illegal and covert regime change missions, civilian PSYOPs, assassinations, and torture both internationally and domestically.

SOCIAL JUSTICE

  • Hold a Constitutional Convention for all territories to ensure full sovereignty for the colonial holdings of American Imperialism.
  • Call for an immediate ceasefire in Palestine and an immediate end to [weapons] funding for Israel. Return to negotiations that recognize Palestinian rights and sovereignty.
  • Hold trials for Joe Biden, Donald Trump, and other politicians who enable genocide and war crimes.
  • End American military neo-colonialism. Deflate military spending, recall overseas forces, and close American bases around the world.
  • Use the budget surplus from such action for social welfare programs to the benefit of the American workers and families.
  • Establish the National American Descendants of Slavery Reparations Investigatory Commission.
  • Establish the National Indigenous American Reparations Investigatory Commission

WORKERS' RIGHTS

  • Protect mass unionization across the country.
